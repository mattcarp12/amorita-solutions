Welcome to Amorita Solutions LLC. We specialize in cloud-native infrastructure, container mechanics, and secure software supply chains.

### About the Practice
Amorita Solutions provides highly specialized software engineering and systems architecture. The practice is structured to engage with complex technical challenges across a variety of operational models, including US Federal Government contracting, direct C2C partnerships (domestic and international), and sustained open-source stewardship. Traditional strategic employment engagements are also considered.

### Featured Focus Areas

* **Cloud-Native & Container Orchestration:** Deep expertise in container internals and automated, GitOps-driven AWS EKS cluster deployments utilizing Terraform, Helm, and ArgoCD.
* **Security & eBPF:** Advanced Kubernetes pod monitoring and sovereignty logic built on Tetragon, abstracting kernel complexity to focus on robust runtime security.
* **Software Supply Chain Optimization:** Research and implementation of non-trivial graph algorithms (such as Dijkstra, Prim's, and Kruskal's) to map, secure, and optimize software dependencies.
* **Policy as Code (Federal & Enterprise):** Development and enforcement of strict compliance policies utilizing Rego, Conftest, and Gatekeeper, tailored for US Federal Government contract requirements.
* **Systems Programming:** Extensive development in Go and C, with an ongoing exploration into minimalist programming language design.
